Our Trips

White Water Thrills: The Rapids Rush

Duration: 1 day Difficulty: Intermediate Description: Join us for an adrenaline-pumping adventure on the rushing rapids of the Wild River. This trip is perfect for thrill-seekers and those with previous rafting experience. Navigate through Class III and IV rapids, with experienced guides leading the way. Between the heart-pounding rapids, enjoy breathtaking views of the surrounding wilderness. Lunch is provided riverside. Safety gear and instruction are included.

Family Fun Float: Scenic River Relaxation

Duration: Half-day Difficulty: Beginner Description: Bring the whole family for a leisurely float down the Calm Creek. This trip is ideal for beginners and families with children aged 6 and above. Enjoy the serene beauty of the river as you gently glide through calm waters. Spot local wildlife and learn about the area's ecology from our knowledgeable guides. A snack and refreshments are provided onboard. Life jackets and basic paddling instruction are included.

Multi-Day Expedition: Wilderness Adventure

Duration: 3 days, 2 nights Difficulty: Advanced Description: Embark on the ultimate rafting adventure through the untamed wilderness of the Deep Gorge. This trip is designed for experienced rafters looking for an immersive outdoor experience. Navigate challenging Class IV and V rapids, camp under the stars along the riverbanks, and explore hidden waterfalls and remote hiking trails. All meals are provided, cooked over an open fire. Experienced guides will lead you through the rugged terrain and ensure your safety at every step. This expedition is for adventurers seeking a true wilderness experience.
